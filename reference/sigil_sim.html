The AI plays both sides with the simulator's greedy heuristic (hard-cast only a T1 base — or a standalone that prints its own play permission, like King Honathan — to the active or passive zone → link equipment immediately to a passive slot → play wars/auras → transform toward terminals → attack to KO else chip the weakest target → elevate the strongest eligible character, from active or passive). T2+ forms are reached only by transformation. Same seed + same decks = same game. Tip: tap or long-press any card on the board (or a card name in the log) to read its full inscription.
